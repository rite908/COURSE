## 🧠 MCQ RULES — BAHUT IMPORTANT — GALTI MAT KARNA

### Rule 1: POSITION RANDOM HONI CHAHIYE
Sahi answer kabhi bhi A, B, C, ya D ho sakta hai. **Sirf B ya C mein mat rakhna.**

**Correct distribution example (15 questions):**
Q1→A, Q2→D, Q3→A, Q4→D, Q5→B, Q6→A, Q7→C, Q8→D, Q9→B, Q10→A, Q11→D, Q12→B, Q13→A, Q14→C, Q15→D

### Rule 2: LENGTH SE SAHI ANSWER GUESS NA HO SAKE
**Sahi answer kabhi kabhi SABSE CHOTA hona chahiye.** Galat options kabhi kabhi sahi se LAMBA hone chahiye.

```
Q: Company apne system ko attack karwati hai permission dekar — kya hai?
A) Penetration Testing                    ← SAHI (short!)
B) Black hat hacking — chahe permission ho, attack ho raha hai...
C) Social engineering — andar ka banda hai isliye insider threat...
D) Grey hat — company ki permission hai lekin third parties ki nahi...
```

### Rule 3: GALAT OPTIONS PLAUSIBLE LAGTE HO
Galat options stupid nahi hone chahiye. Logically reasonable lagte hon.

### Rule 4: EXPLANATION CONCISE RAKHO
Sahi jawab ke baad 1-2 line explanation — zyada mat likho.
